HLS vs. WebRTC: which should I use?

HLS: works everywhere with no extra network setup, and tolerates unstable connections gracefully by buffering and catching up - but has a 5-10 second delay.

WebRTC: sub-second delay, best for "who's at the door right now" - but needs network port 8555 reachable for remote/off-network viewing, is less forgiving of packet loss (can stutter instead of buffering), and uses a bit more server load per concurrent viewer.
